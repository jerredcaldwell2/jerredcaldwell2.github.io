2014 - 2022: Manager - Classy Closets

In 2014, I started as an installer, installing a variety of custom cabinets.

Worked on residential and commercial projects.

In 2015, I engineered a full production line and became the primary operator for all manufacturing.

Served on the design team and engineered project blueprints to customer specifications.

Created standard production procedures and maintenance guidelines.

Started training other machine operators on our process and increased efficiencies.

In 2018, I developed the company IT strategy and integrated the network of computers and machinery.

Was the admin of the Ubiquiti network and Microsoft cloud systems.

In 2020, I took the time to teach myself Python and other computer skills to utilize in manufacturing.

Created an inventory barcode system and program for production engineering using Python.

Architected and engineered an Enterprise Resource Planner (ERP) using Django, Python, and AWS.

Troubleshot problems and maximized efficiency and scalability of ERP server.
